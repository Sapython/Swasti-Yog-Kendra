    Overview

    Swasti Yog Kendra's 4-day yoga retreat in Rishikesh provides an immersive and rejuvenating experience for attendees. During the retreat, participants will have the opportunity to deepen their yoga practice, connect with nature, and engage in activities that promote overall wellness. The retreat includes daily yoga classes led by experienced instructors, guided meditations, and nature walks. Additionally, attendees will have the chance to enjoy healthy meals, take part in workshops and discussions, and engage in various outdoor activities.
